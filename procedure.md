<b>Requirements (Instruments, Chemicals & Other)</b> :<br> 
•	Stainless steel Pontoon: Size 300 x 150 mm (Approx.> with a Horizontal Guide Bar for sliding weight).<br>
•	A set of different weights.<br>
•	Water Tank with Front Window of Tank : made of Glass / Perspex<br>

<b>Procedure</b> :<br> 
1. Make the tank free from dust.<br>
2. Fill tank ¾ with clean water and ensure that no foreign particles are there.<br>
3. Weigh the ship model to find W.<br>
4. Float the ship model in water and ensure that it is in stable equilibrium.<br>
5. Apply the known weight (w) at the center of model.<br>
6. Give the model a small angular displacement in clockwise direction.<br>
7. Measure the distance moved by the weight applied with the help of scale.<br>
8. Measure the angle of tilt on the graduated arc<br>
9. Repeat the experiment for different weights.<br>
